Ingredients!

  • Leftover Rice
  • Half clove of garlic
  • Any Cooking oil
  • Any leftover meat

Steps on how to cook!

  1. Grab your leftover rice from the fridge
  2. Chop a half clove of garlic
  3. Heat up a pan to medium hit and apply any cooking oil
  4. Toss the garlic into the pan wait for it to toast for a little. If toasted, dunk your rice into the pan!
  5. Apply your desired ammount of salt, Toss the rice for 3-10 minutes until cooked!
  6. Re-heat your any leftrover meat and plate it with the fried rice
